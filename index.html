The Longhouse

We are getting married at The Longhouse at Mill on the Brue in Bruton, in the heart of rural Somerset. The Mill on the Brue's day job is a children's outdoor activty centre.

There are links on the Longhouse website to local B&Bs and also information on how to get there.

Accommodation

We will be staying at the Longhouse in the 'Bridal Flat' (where the teachers sleep when their school's visiting the activity centre). There is accommodation for about 50 people on site in dorm rooms - think school field trips! If you don't fancy the trek home after the wedding this is a cheap and cheerful option as B&B is £20pp.
